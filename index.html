GlobalChat

Global Chat lets users communicate across different servers in a shared channel. Messages are only visible to those who have selected the same language, ensuring clear and relevant conversations. Connect with like-minded users, make new friends, and engage in discussions effortlessly.

LingoWithMe (Development)

LingoWithMe is an upcoming feature designed to make language learning fun and engaging directly on Discord. Every day, users will receive a new challenge in their selected learning language. By completing these challenges correctly, they can build a streak, earn potential rewards, and unlock special badges. This feature helps you stay consistent and motivated while improving your language skills. Stay tuned for its launch!

Easy Setup Feature

Our bot is designed for maximum ease of use with the /hello command. Every setup step for its features is handled through this single command, making the process simple and intuitive. No complicated configurations—just a smooth and user-friendly experience right from the start.
